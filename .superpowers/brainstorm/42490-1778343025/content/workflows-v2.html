Filter Definitions

Platform — Multi-select: Instagram, TikTok, Douyin, YouTube, X/Twitter, etc.
Location — Two-level: Country → Region/State. Filter by where the influencer is based.
Followers — Min/max slider or preset ranges (1K-10K, 10K-50K, 50K-100K, 100K-500K, 500K-1M, 1M+)
Scope (Tier) — Based on follower count:
• Nano: 1K – 10K
• Micro: 10K – 100K
• Macro: 100K – 1M
• Mega: 1M+
Keywords — Matches influencer bio, niche category, and display name/handle.
